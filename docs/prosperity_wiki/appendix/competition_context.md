## Prosperity

- Prosperity 4 is IMC's global online trading challenge.
- It is designed for university students who want to get familiar with algorithmic trading and financial markets.
- The goal is to earn as many XIRECs as possible.
- The challenge runs for 16 days in total.
- The final winner is the team that has generated the most profit of all teams.

## Story context

- Players travel to Intara.
- XIREN means eXtended Interplanetary Resource Exchange Network.
- The storyline says the team is setting up a trading outpost to help turn Intara's raw potential into profitable trading activities.
- The storyline states that securing at least 200,000 XIRECs by the end of trading Round 2 makes the mission a success.

## IMC

- IMC is a global research-driven quantitative trading firm.
- The raw page says IMC has 10 global offices and more than 1900 employees.
- Listed offices: Aarhus, Amsterdam, Chicago, Hong Kong, London, Mumbai, New York, Seoul, Sydney, and Zug.

## Participation and teams

- The platform is open to anyone for learning purposes.
- Eligibility for leaderboard ranking, prizes, and recognition has additional requirements.
- Signup must be completed before Round 1 begins at 12:00 CEST (UTC+02:00) on April 14, 2026.
- Team changes are possible through the end of Round 2.
- Team formation is locked when Round 2 closes.
- A participant may not be a member of more than one team.

## Eligibility and disqualification

The raw sources include requirements for:

- university enrollment
- age
- ineligible affiliations
- previous Top 10 participation
- verification calls for Top 5 Teams and Manual Trading Winner
- prize residency restrictions
- legal, reputational, or compliance concerns
- exploitation of platform bugs
- plagiarism or unauthorized use of another participant's trading strategies, algorithms, or code

For legal and prize details, refer back to the raw terms file.
