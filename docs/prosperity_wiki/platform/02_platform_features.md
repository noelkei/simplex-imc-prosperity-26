# Platform Features

Source basis: `docs/prosperity_wiki_raw/05_game_mechanics_overview.md`.

## A.R.I.A. Uplinks

- A.R.I.A. Uplinks are the main source of information for Prosperity.
- A.R.I.A. stands for Autonomous Relay Interface Assistant.
- A.R.I.A. guides algorithmic and manual trading activities.
- A new Uplink is available at the beginning of every round.

## Outpost View

- Outpost View provides an overview of the team's outpost and unlocked structures.
- The outpost grows as the team generates XIRECS profit.
- It shows team name, PnL indicator, and overall leaderboard rank.
- It contains direct links to the Leaderboard and Crew Honors overview.
- Mission Control is at the bottom of Outpost View.

## Leaderboard

- The Leaderboard ranks all competing teams in Prosperity 4.
- Overall ranking shows total PnL per team.
- The Algorithmic tab shows ranking by algorithmic trading performance.
- The Manual tab shows ranking by manual trading performance.
- The Country tab shows the team's ranking compared with other teams using the same team country.

## Crew Honors

- Teams can earn badges for actions and achievements.
- Collected and uncollected badges are visible on the Crew Honors page.
- Crew Honors is accessible through Outpost View or the main menu.
- Badges can be shared by clicking a badge and using the share buttons in the description.

## On-Board Advisors

- Teams can choose one of three On-Board Advisors during the Tutorial Round.
- Teams may switch Advisors while the Tutorial Round lasts.
- When the Tutorial Round ends, the Advisor selection is locked.
- From Round 1 onward, Advisors provide perspectives and guidance for challenges.
- The Advisor is located in the bottom right corner of Outpost View.
